# Privacy Policy for De Vibe OMS

**Last Updated: June 14, 2026**

DavinciShah ("we", "us", or "our") operates the De Vibe Order Management System (OMS) desktop application and associated services (collectively, the "Application"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our Application.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects and processes information to provide, maintain, and improve our services:

### A. Account Information
* When you sign up or log in (via email and password or Google Single Sign-On), we collect your **email address**, **username**, and **profile information** to authenticate your account.

### B. Business Data
* To manage your inventory, orders, billing, and shipping, the Application processes transaction-related details, including product inventories, customer names, contact numbers, order histories, billing details, and tax codes (e.g. GST).
* This data is stored locally on your machine or on your configured backend database server.

### C. Technical Data
* We do not collect telemetry, crash reports, or application usage diagnostics unless explicitly consented to.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party service providers to facilitate Application features:
* **Google SSO:** Used to verify your identity and allow secure login. No profile data other than your email and name is processed.
* **Stripe:** Used for billing integrations. We do not store credit card numbers or raw payment credentials.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely:
* Local configuration data (such as API keys and settings) is stored encrypted in your local application directory (`%APPDATA%/De Vibe OMS/`).
* Business data is stored on your designated server backend database. We do not host or store your transactional data on our own servers.

---

## 4. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as GDPR or CCPA), you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your account information. Since your business data is hosted on your own database server, you retain full ownership and control over the deletion of your inventory and sales records.
