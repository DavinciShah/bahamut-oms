Personal Data We Collect

In accordance with the principle of data minimisation under the DPDP Act, 2023, we collect only such personal data as is necessary for the stated purpose.

A. Identity & Account Data

  • Email address (for account creation and Google SSO login)
  • Name or username (as provided during registration)
  • Hashed authentication credentials (passwords are never stored in plaintext)

B. Business & Transactional Data

This data is entered directly by you and stored on your own designated database server:

  • Customer names, contact numbers, and email addresses
  • Order details, product inventory records, billing amounts
  • GST numbers, invoice metadata, and shipping information

C. Technical Configuration Data

  • Runtime configuration (API host, port — stored locally in %APPDATA%/De Vibe OMS/)
  • Session tokens (stored in memory; cleared on logout)

D. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect telemetry, crash dumps, or diagnostic reports
  • We do not collect biometric data, financial account numbers, or Aadhaar numbers
  • We do not track your browsing behaviour or location

Data Storage & Security

In accordance with Section 8(5) of the DPDP Act, 2023, we implement reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Security Measures

  • Passwords: Hashed using bcrypt with a salt factor of 12 — never stored in plaintext
  • API Authentication: JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with short-lived expiry windows
  • Local Configuration: Runtime configuration stored in user-space directory (%APPDATA%) with OS-level access controls
  • Transport Security: All communications between the desktop client and backend use HTTPS / secure WebSockets
  • CORS Enforcement: Backend restricts API access to trusted origins only
🗄️
Data Location: Your business data (orders, inventory, customers) is stored on your own self-hosted database server. DavinciShah does not host, access, or own a copy of your transactional data.

Data Retention & Erasure

Under Section 8(7) of the DPDP Act, 2023, personal data must not be retained beyond the period necessary for the specified purpose.

  • Account Credentials: Retained for the lifetime of your account and deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Session Tokens: Stored in memory only; automatically cleared on logout or session expiry.
  • Business Data: Stored entirely on your own database server — you have full control and responsibility for retention and deletion.
  • Support Correspondence: Any emails or queries sent to us are retained for up to 12 months for grievance resolution purposes.

To request deletion of your account data, email us at devibe70@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Erasure Request".
